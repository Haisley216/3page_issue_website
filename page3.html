Who's story?

Mi Ryeong Kim (Andong House)

Her life story in Andong Noodle

Her family was well-off as a child, but the sudden failure of her father's business left them poor. The shock left him with a cerebral hemorrhage and semi-incapacitated, and her mother started selling noodles in a corner of the market to make a living. As a child, she hated noodles because she thought they represented her poverty, but her mother's health deteriorated and she learned to cook them. Her noodle business gave her a husband, a child, a family, and a better livelihood. The noodles that she used to hate to eat are now the best thing that has helped her and her family live a happy life.
